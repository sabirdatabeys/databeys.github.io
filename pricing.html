Frequently asked questions

How does DataBeys work?

Databeys is a B2B digital agency. We offer CRM, customer support, digital marketing, web development, cloud support and hosted email services on a monthly subscription basis. Our team of experts is readily available at your service and will provide you with any kind of support you require. To learn more about what we do, please visit our services page.

Do I have to pay for the software?

No, the software is included with our subscription. You just have to pay for your services.You can visit our services and pricing page for more information.

How much do your services cost?

There are two ways you can work with us: Monthly Retainer package including: -12 month minimum engagement, Website Development, Customer Support, CRM and Marketing

Fixed Project-One time payment, Business emails, Website Development, CRM implementation

The price can vary depending on the package you want to subscribe to. But you can avail our services at as low as 1500 AED. You can check the prices and further details of our packages here.

Do I have to pay upfront or monthly?

For our Monthly retainer package you have to pay monthly. For our Fixed Package, you have to pay upfront. You can check the prices and further details of our packages here.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

For our monthly retainer package, the minimum engagement time is 12 months. In order to cancel your subscription, you have to provide a 2 months advance notice. If you cancel without a notice or before the contract is finished, you will lose access to your account, software and will be unable to migrate your data.

Is your CRM a good fit for my business?

Both large scale and small businesses can benefit from DataBeys's CRM services. You can request a demo to learn more about our services.
